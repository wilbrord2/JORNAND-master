ABOUT THE COURSE

Grow your beginner and advanced-level Revit skills for higher quality and better coordinated architectural design, construction, and structural engineering through our Autodesk authorized Revit training courses. Learn Autodesk Revit from the industry experts and use the building information modelling (BIM) application to design a house, building, or other structure.

The Revit training will prepare you for designing, collaborating, visualizing vital building elements, delivering best-in-class product models, and efficiently using Revit, the multidisciplinary BIM software. For this course, you will get to learn new tricks and practices about Revit to develop new skills and increase efficiency.

Autodesk Revit FAQs

What is Autodesk Revit?

Revit, by Autodesk, is a building information modelling (BIM) software developed for Microsoft Windows and enables users to design with drafting elements and parametric modelling. Revit delivers dynamic information in intelligent models that accurately design complex building structures and document them quickly.

Who should attend the Autodesk Revit course?

The courses we offer are aligned with the relevant Autodesk certifications. We recommend you choose a course relevant to your skills and area(s) of expertise. Some of the typical roles associated with Autodesk Revit are:

  • General Engineer Designer
  • Interior Designer
  • Architect
  • Landscape Architect
  • Structural Engineer
  • MEP Engineer
  • Contractor
  • BIM Manager
